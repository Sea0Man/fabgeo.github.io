Tbilisi



  Welcome to our extraordinary Tbilisi Tour, a captivating journey through time and culture that will immerse you in the heart of Georgia's dynamic capital city.Tbilisi is a city that seamlessly blends its ancient past with a modern, cosmopolitan atmosphere. Stroll through the charming streets of the old town, where you'll encounter a fusion of architectural styles, including the iconic Narikala Fortress, Sioni Cathedral, and the Bridge of Peace. Delve into the city's history at the National Museum of Georgia or indulge in the local cuisine at the bustling food markets and traditional restaurants.







Mtatsminda Park



  

Our adventure begins at Mtatsminda Park, a beloved landmark perched on the lush slopes of Mount Mtatsminda. As we ascend to this picturesque park, you'll be enchanted by the panoramic views of Tbilisi stretching before you. The history of Mtatsminda dates back to the 19th century when it served as a leisure retreat for Georgia's nobility. Today, it's a vibrant amusement park, offering thrilling rides and a joyful atmosphere for visitors of all ages.








Kartlis Deda



  Next, we'll encounter the iconic Mother of Georgia statue - "Kartlis deda"(mother of Kartli/Georgia), proudly standing as a symbol of the nation's resilience and strength. Gazing over the city, this majestic figure holds a bowl of wine for friends and a sword for foes, encapsulating the essence of Georgian hospitality and determination.









Narikala Fortress




  Prepare to be transported back in time as we venture to Narikala Fortress, an ancient citadel that has guarded Tbilisi for centuries. Its origins can be traced to the 4th century, and the fortress has witnessed numerous historical events. As we explore its ancient walls and towers, you'll feel a connection to the past and an appreciation for the strategic importance of this historic site.






Metekhi



  Continuing our journey, we'll visit Metekhi, an iconic district with a history dating back to the 12th century. The Metekhi Church, standing on a rocky outcrop, is a masterpiece of Georgian architecture and a symbol of the city's spiritual heritage. Its location, overlooking the Kura River, offers a breathtaking view of Tbilisi's modern skyline.








The Bridge of Peace



  Crossing the Bridge of Peace, a contemporary architectural wonder, we'll witness the harmonious fusion of old and new in Tbilisi. This modern pedestrian bridge spans the Kura River and is adorned with stunning glass and steel designs. Its creation marked a pivotal moment in Tbilisi's urban development, connecting the historic district with the modern cityscape.







  So, join us on this unforgettable Tbilisi Tour, where history and modernity intertwine, and where the essence of Georgia's capital will leave an indelible mark on your heart.




Our team of professional guides, with their deep knowledge and passion for Georgia's history and culture, will be your companions on this extraordinary expedition.
