Kakheti



  Welcome to our delightful Kakheti Tour, a captivating journey through the enchanting region of Eastern Georgia, renowned for its picturesque landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and world-class wines!







Sighnagi



  

Our first stop is the charming town of Sighnaghi, often referred to as the "City of Love." As we stroll through the cobblestone streets and admire the pastel-colored houses, you'll feel the romantic ambiance that has earned this town its nickname. Enjoy breathtaking views of the Alazani Valley from the town's ancient city walls, adding a touch of magic to your experience.








Bodme Church



  Next, we'll visit the serene Bodme church, nestled amidst lush vineyards and rolling hills. This peaceful sanctuary offers a moment of tranquility, where you can appreciate the harmonious connection between nature and spirituality









Alazani Valley




  As we continue our journey through the fertile Alazani Valley, prepare your taste buds for a true indulgence. Kakheti is renowned for its exceptional wines, and we have arranged a wine tasting experience that will delight your senses. Sample a variety of fine wines, from the robust reds to the crisp whites, and learn about the traditional winemaking methods that have been passed down through generations






Degustation



  But that's not all; our tour also includes a delectable cheese and bread tasting, where you can savor the flavors of locally produced artisanal cheeses paired with freshly baked bread. And to add a touch of Georgian spirit, we invite you to sample Georgian Chacha, a traditional grape brandy known for its strong character.








Telavi



  Our final stop is the historical town of Telavi, the heart of Kakheti. Immerse yourself in the town's rich heritage as we explore its charming streets and learn about the influential figures who once called Telavi home.

Our aim is to provide you with an authentic and immersive experience, one that allows you to connect with the soul of Georgia. So, join us on this delightful Kakheti Tour, where love, culture, and the spirit of Georgia converge. Let's raise our glasses in celebration of life, love, and the beauty that surrounds us in this enchanting corner of the world




Our team of professional guides, with their deep knowledge and passion for Georgia's history and culture, will be your companions on this extraordinary expedition.
