Batumi



  Let's head to the coastal city of Batumi, a vibrant destination on the Black Sea coast. Batumi is renowned for its modern architecture, lively atmosphere, and beautiful seaside promenade. Take a leisurely stroll along the Batumi Boulevard, adorned with sculptures, fountains, and colorful flower beds. Marvel at the futuristic Batumi Tower, visit the Batumi Botanical Garden, or try your luck at the elegant Batumi Casino.







Coastal gem of Batumi



  

Our journey takes us from the vibrant city of Tbilisi to the stunning coastal gem of Batumi, nestled along the Black Sea. As we leave the capital behind, the picturesque landscapes of Georgia will unfold before your eyes, taking you through charming villages, lush green valleys, and majestic mountain ranges.








Batumi Boulevard



  Batumi, often referred to as the "Pearl of the Black Sea," is a coastal paradise that will captivate you with its unique blend of modern architecture and historic charm. As we approach the city, you'll be greeted by Batumi's iconic landmarks, such as the Batumi Boulevard, stretching along the coast with lush gardens and recreational spaces.









Batumi Botanical Garden




  Explore Batumi's vibrant city center, where contemporary skyscrapers stand alongside charming old buildings adorned with intricate architectural details. You'll have the opportunity to visit the Batumi Botanical Garden, one of the largest and most diverse botanical gardens in the world, boasting a stunning collection of plants from various regions.
